About Me

Hi, I'm Kai Francis, a Computer Science major at the College of Wooster with a passion for problem-solving, cybersecurity, and accessibility in tech. With experience in programming languages like Python, C/C++, and JavaScript, I enjoy building user-friendly and secure software solutions.

My work includes developing tools like a WCAG Color Contrast Grader for accessibility, designing a Student Planner for organizational efficiency, and simulating real-world cyberattacks in educational projects to improve security measures. As a student of Africana Studies, I bring a unique perspective to my work, exploring how technology intersects with culture and community.

Beyond coding, I’m a dedicated learner in the fields of machine learning, web development, and cybersecurity, constantly exploring ways to make technology more inclusive and impactful. Let’s connect to collaborate on exciting projects!
